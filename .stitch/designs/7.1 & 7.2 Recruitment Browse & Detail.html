Description

The Global BioTech Sprint is an intensive 48-hour hackathon where students collaborate to solve real-world laboratory bottlenecks. This year's focus is on "The Virtual Lab"—creating digital twins and interactive interfaces for expensive lab hardware that often has limited access.

We are looking for a student designer to join a team of three bio-engineers. Your role will be to translate complex data visualizations and equipment control flows into a clean, modern user interface. You will be responsible for high-fidelity prototyping and the final presentation deck's visual storytelling.
